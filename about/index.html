Whales R us? What about it?

Whales R us is a non profit

We are a nonprofit organization seeking to return whales to their natural habitat in the African Desert. The reason the Desert is dry is because there are no longer any whales to take care of watering the ground. The spouts of the whales is used to irrigate the land and will hopefully one day return prosperity to African soil.

How can you help?

Go to your locale charities and ask them if they support Whales R Us if they don't have them reach out to us. If they do donate to them and help them to further the goals of the Whales R Us initiative.
